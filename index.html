Backpacking Gear: What You Need!

There is something amazing about exploring the outdoors. Fresh air, wild animals, and not a city nearby for dozens or hundreds of miles. Figuring out what you need to start seems intimidating at first, since backpacking is physically demanding and requires careful planning on what essential piece of equipment should be brought each trip to keep weight as low as possible. However, keep reading and you'll learn exactly which gear you need to start your adventure!

The Big Three

The top three most important pieces of gear. Never forget these!

  • Backpack 40L or 60L Size
  • Tent
  • Sleeping Bag and sleeping pad

Essential Items

Just as important, you'll be using these throughout your trip!

  • Hiking boots
  • Stove and fuel canister
  • Water bladder or bottle
  • Sun protection(sunglasses, sunscreen, hat)
  • First aid kit
  • Hygiene supplies(toothbrush, toiletries, etc)
  • Weather-appropriate clothes
  • Fleece and/or Down jacket
